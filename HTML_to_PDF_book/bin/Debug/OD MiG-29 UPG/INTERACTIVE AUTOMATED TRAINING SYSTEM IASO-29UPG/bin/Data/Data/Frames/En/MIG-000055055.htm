IASO-29UPG is intended for theoretical and practical training of flight and engineering maintenance personnel and for resolution of the complex of tasks related to operation and maintenance of MiG-29UPG aircrafts.

IASO-29UPG is of modular design, and its computing environment has open architecture. IASO-29UPG ensures training of aviation personnel in accordance with Maintenance Manual, Flight Manual.

 

IASO-29UPG provides the following for technical-and-engineering personnel:

  • study of:
    • structure, components, performance data, operation principle of airframe, engine, aircraft systems, radioelectronic and avionic equipment and armament;
    • controls and their arrangement in the cockpit;
    • maintenance of the aircraft;
    • troubleshooting.
  • conceptual exercise of practical execution of the following work types:
    • serviceability check of aircraft systems and equipment within the scope of flight preparations;
    • evaluation of technical state of aircraft systems and equipment;
    • operation with engine, systems, equipment and armament controls;
    • starting and running-up of the engines.

For flight personnel IASO-29UPG ensures the following:

  • study of:
    • practical aircraft aerodynamics;
    • airframe, systems and equipment of the aircraft;
    •  operation during special cases in flight in accordance with Flight Manual.
  • conceptual exercise of procedures with controls in the cockpit during flight preparation;

 

IASO-29UPG provides the following for the instructor:

  • carrying out of training and individual lessons in the conditions of group and individual training;
  • preparation of personal training courses and individual lessons;
  • immediate selection of training programs and objects of study;
  • documenting of training results in IASO-29UPG database;
  • automated monitoring and evaluation of the trainees level of training;
  • documenting of training results in the database.

For system programmer the IASO-29UPG ensures the following:

  • registration of trainees and instructors in the training computer class database;
  • control of access to the automated training courses of trainees and instructors groups;
  • carrying out of methodical work on upgrading of the training course (in association with instructor);
  • preparation of multimedia and video lectures (in association with instructor);
  • backing-up and recovery of data of the training computer class software.
